This Privacy Policy explains how Mosque (the app) and RK&RS INNOVATIONS LTD ('Mosque', 'we', 'us', or
'our') collect, use, share, and protect information when you use the app and related services.
This policy is written to reflect the features and data flows currently implemented in the app codebase,
including profile features, ibadah features, admin workflows, notifications, adverts, and account controls. It is
not a replacement for legal adviceF.
1. Information We Collect
- We collect information you provide directly when you create or use a Mosque account, including profile details
(such as display name, phone number, email address, mosque selection, and certain settings), content you
submit, and requests you make through the app.
- We collect app usage and technical information needed to operate and secure the service, including a device
identifier (stored by the app), app version, device/app metadata, network type, and service usage events. We
may also collect push notification tokens and notification permission status so we can deliver notifications and
manage your preferences.
- If you use specific features, we may collect feature-specific data. Examples include ibadah task progress
(personal, partner, or ummah flows), prayer and reading progress, service bookings or service requests,
registrations, feedback submissions, advert inquiries, and other user-generated content.
- Some features involve sensitive information. For example, Hayd and Nifas features require explicit feature
consent and are handled as sensitive information. We only process such data for the feature's purpose and
related safety, support, and legal needs.
- Camera and microphone access are used only when you choose features that require them (for example, photo
capture or adhaan recording). If a verification photo flow is used, the app may capture and store a face photo for
verification workflow purposes. Mosque does not use facial recognition for identity matching and does not create
biometric identification templates. If on-device face detection is used, it is used only for camera
framing/alignment and not for recognizing who you are.
- Location access is optional and feature-dependent. The code supports optional location collection for device
metadata or localized functionality, but location is not required for core use of the app.
- Advertising and analytics data: when adverts are shown, the app may request adverts using contextual inputs
such as placement, mosque context, and region context. The app may record advert interaction events (such as
impressions, clicks, dismissals) and may hash device identifiers server-side for privacy-safe analytics. At the
time of this policy, Mosque does not use cross-app tracking or ATT-based personalized ad tracking unless we
separately request permission and update this policy.
- Cookies: the mobile app itself does not rely on browser cookies. If you use a web version of Mosque, browser
cookies or similar storage may be used for essential functionality. Any non-essential web tracking or cookie use
would be disclosed separately.
2. How We Use Information
- We use information to provide, maintain, and improve Mosque features, including account access,
mosque-scoped features, ibadah features, notifications, service bookings and requests, admin workflows,
support, and safety controls.
- We use profile and account information to identify your account, connect your app activity to your account, apply
mosque-specific rules, and deliver the features you request.

3. Sharing Information
- We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as needed to operate Mosque, provide
requested features, comply with law, and protect users and the service.
- Mosque admins and central admins may access member information that is required for mosque administration,
support, verification, moderation, service operations, or related community management features. Regular
members generally do not have a general member-profile browsing feature in the app.
- Feature-based sharing may occur by design. For example, partner task participants can see each other's
participant identifiers and limited partner labels in partner task flows; ummah features may display aggregate or
shared totals.
- We use service providers and infrastructure processors to operate Mosque (for example, cloud hosting,
databases, file storage, authentication, push messaging, and related support services). These providers process
information on our behalf under applicable terms and security controls.
- We may share information with regulators, law enforcement, courts, or other authorities where required by law,
legal process, or where reasonably necessary to protect rights, safety, or the integrity of Mosque.
- If we reorganize, merge, sell assets, or transfer the service, relevant information may be transferred as part of
that transaction subject to applicable law.
- If you tap an advert or external link, the destination website or service is controlled by a third party. Their privacy
practices apply to your use of their services.
4. How Long We Keep Information
- We keep information for as long as needed to provide the service, maintain security and records, resolve
disputes, and meet legal, tax, accounting, or regulatory obligations.
- Account and profile information is generally retained while your account is active. Some operational logs, audit
records, and security data may be kept longer where needed for fraud prevention, abuse prevention, and
compliance.
- Feature data (for example, tasks, bookings, registrations, notifications, or support records) may have different
retention periods depending on the feature and its operational purpose.
- Advert analytics and event data may be retained in raw or aggregated form for reporting, fraud prevention, and
service improvement. Where possible, data may be reduced, aggregated, or de-identified.
- If you request account deletion, we will process deletion in line with our systems and legal obligations. Some
deletion flows may include a temporary recovery window before permanent deletion. Certain data may be
retained where required by law or necessary to protect the service and users.

undermine security).
- We reserve the right to enforce our Terms, moderate content, suspend or restrict misuse, and keep records
necessary to protect the Mosque community, our systems, and our legal rights.
7. Mosque's Audience
- Mosque is a community and mosque-services app. It is not designed as an open social profile-browsing network.
- Regular members generally cannot browse other members' full profiles through a general member directory.
Member details may be accessible to mosque admins and central admins where needed for mosque
administration, moderation, support, or service workflows.
- Some features intentionally share limited participant information (for example, partner-task and random-match
flows) so the feature can function.
- Mosque is not intended for children under 13, and if local law sets a higher minimum age, that higher age
applies. If we learn we collected personal information from a child in violation of applicable law, we will take
reasonable steps to delete it.
